THE OIL AND GAS INDUSTRY

Oil plays an important role in the global economy despite the continued efforts to reduce its use and to find alternative green energy sources.
In the early days, finding oil during a drill was considered somewhat of a nuisance as the intended treasures were normally water or salt. It wasn't until 1846 that the first commercial oil well was drilled in the Absheron Peninsula, Azerbaijan (Mir, 2021).
