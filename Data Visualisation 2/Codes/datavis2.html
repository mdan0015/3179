THE OIL AND GAS INDUSTRY

Oil plays an important role in the global economy despite the continued efforts to reduce its use and to find alternative green energy sources.
In the early days, finding oil during a drill was considered somewhat of a nuisance as the intended treasures were normally water or salt. It wasn't until 1846 that the first commercial oil well was drilled in the Absheron Peninsula, Azerbaijan (Mir, 2021).

Oil Reserves Per Capita

The term oil reserves refers to an estimate of the amount of crude oil located in a particular economic region with the potential of being extracted. Crude oil can be referred to as petroleum.

Crude oil is a naturally occurring fossil fuel - meaning it comes from the remains of dead organisms. It's made up of a mixture of hydrocarbons - hydrogen and carbon atoms, in liquid form in underground reservoirs within sedimentary rock. Alternatively, it can be found near the surface in oil sands, hence, their overwhelming presence in the Middle-East.
